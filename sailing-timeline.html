Sailing Journey

My sailing adventure began in 2023 with the RYA Day Skipper qualification, opening up a world of ocean exploration and maritime skills.

RYA Day Skipper Qualification

UKSA Isle of Wight → Solent → Return
📅 September 2023 ⚓ 120 nm ⏱️ 5 days
RYA Day Skipper is a practical sailing qualification that enables you to safely skipper a yacht up to 20m in familiar waters during daylight hours. The course covers boat handling under sail and power, navigation, pilotage, meteorology, rules of the road, and safety procedures.

Completed at the prestigious UKSA Isle of Wight in the challenging waters of the Solent. This qualification provided the foundation skills and confidence needed for independent sailing adventures.
